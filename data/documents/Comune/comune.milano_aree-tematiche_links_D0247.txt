Milan, Paris, Warsaw: conversation on climate-responsive urban regeneration
Curated by C40 Cities
L'evento sarà in lingua inglese. The event will be held in english.
At a time when new urban models are needed to tackle climate change, discussions between European cities must be strengthened and good regeneration practices disseminated.
This session, curated by C40 Cities, will highlight the connection between Milan, Paris and Warsaw. The three major European metropolises will share their common issues and specific challenges as well as the experiences they are implementing to adjust their way of planning and designing cities and to better adapt our urban environment to global warming and modern challenges.
With:
Hélène Chartier – Director Urban Planning and Design – C40 Cities
Charles Antoine Depardon - Advisor for Urban Planning and Architecture to the First Deputy Mayor, Paris
Monika Konrad - Deputy Chief Architect, Warsaw
Giancarlo Tancredi – Deputy Mayor for Urban Regeneration, Milan
